Porkchops

Description

These scrumptious delicious porkchops are ready to rest easy on your tongue and in your belly. Many receipt followers have almost died at the thought of being able to taste these delicious chops.

While you are preparing this meal please understand that this is not a real recipe and you should not actually follow these dumbfounding steps.

Ingredients

  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Olive Oil
  • Pork Chops
  • Some Sauce

Steps

  1. Crack Knuckles
  2. Go get the Pan
  3. Place on cooking eye at Medium Low Temperature
  4. Drizzle Olive Oil on pan while heating
  5. Season Porkchops with Salt and Pepper
  6. Cook Porkchops 1 at a time for 4 mins on each side
  7. Place on Dinner Plate and ENJOY!!
